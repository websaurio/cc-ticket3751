The Villa La Zagaleta

A modern villa on one of the remaining premier plots in the world renowned private estate of La Zagaleta near Marbella.

The original brief from our client was very detailed. A versatile home suitable for a family or a couple. An inside-outside feel, incorporating extensive use of glass and wood, were key requirements of the brief for a modern villa with Asian architectural influences.

Villa Torreblanca

The clients for this villa were very definite on what they wanted - key points in the brief were for a light filled, modern villa.

The unique design feature is the juxtaposition of the first floor which has been rotated through 45º over the L-shaped ground floor to enable sea views from the Master Bedroom and a balcony giving shade to the lounge area below. A double height atrium incorporates the staircase with a picture window.

Villa Caracola

A very individual modern villa showcasing our unique design capabilities. Sustainability features have been included for clients.

The impressive entrance to Villa Caracola is via a cube 'floating on a water bed'. The hall is a double-height atrium, from where the open-plan living area is accessed. A cantilevered staircase leads to the first floor with three en-suite bedrooms. A modern take on the galleried landing leads across a bridge to an impressive suite.
